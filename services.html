Surface Wiring System:

It's a low cost and yet effective wiring system where wires or pipes are installated or mounted directly on a finished wall surface. The surface wiring system has thus been known to have an easy-maintenance advantage for most new and experienced technicians when detecting fault and determining damage rate, thus helping them make quick decisions in offering technical solutions. Though many tend to waiver the use of surface systems but has ignored the quality and standards that makes the usage as effective as other major wiring like conduit system.

Concealed Conduit Wiring System:

Concealed Wiring System is a system where walls are carved in to allow path for conduit pipes creating pathways for cables and connections. The endpoint beauty of the conduit system has made it popular in many countries. Though physical maintenance observation is mostly difficult when trouleshooting, many who heed to the quality and standard required has made conduit the benefits pick for both commercial and industrial needs.

Trunking Wiring System:

Trunking wiring system is system designed to conceal wires and cables in a covered container. The ease of maintaining this system of wiring has made it more effective in industrial area where troubleshooting time should be limited and swift. A good trunking system done with measurement and alignment in mind creates a very beautiful structure especially in residential homes.
Trunking system has also been an alternative restoration type of wiring for blocked conduit pipes in applications where pipes pathway gets blocked.

Solar Power System:

True to all wiring systems, solar system is offered to provide alternative power to curb the irregularity in public power supply, save the carbon pollution and support the increase in clean energy. Our solar installation are prepared to be safe and user friendly with clients need and taste in mind.

Service Process:

An electrical service process refers to the set of procedures and protocols that are followed by electricians or electrical service providers when providing electrical services to their clients. The electrical service process typically involves several steps, These includes: Assessment, Planning and estimating, Testing, Inspection and cleanup, Maintenance
Overall, the electrical service process is designed to ensure that electrical work is completed safely, efficiently, and to the satisfaction of the client.


Maintenance Service Benefits

Every home and business needs electricity, which is why regular maintenance & upkeep is so important. Your car requires regular servicing to keep it running smoothly. In a similar way, regular electrical maintenance is important as it provides an opportunity to catch any issues before they become a real problem, such as nuisance tripping or in more extreme cases- fire.
Make sure that your wiring, outlets, switches, lights & switchboards are all working as they should! Most people think that as long as their lights & powerpoints work that there are no issues, but there could be some underlying problems.

Below are benefits of a regular electrical maintenance service:

- Preventing Expensive Problems
- Protecting Electrical Appliances
- Minimizing Fire Hazards, Burns and Shocks
- Saving Cost and Time
- Lower Energy Consumption and Efficiency
- Extending Lifespan of The Wiring System
- Clients Safety and Peace of Mind
